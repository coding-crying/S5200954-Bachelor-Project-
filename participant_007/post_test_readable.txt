PART A: MULTIPLE CHOICE
Choose the word that best completes each sentence.

1. The professor's _____ explanation of quantum mechanics left most students confused.
   a) abstruse  b) ephemeral  c) deleterious  d) perfunctory

2. The teacher had to _____ the students for their disruptive behavior during the assembly.
   a) capitulate  b) bolster  c) admonish  d) cajole

3. The monk lived an _____ lifestyle, rejecting all material comforts.
   a) austere  b) capricious  c) garrulous  d) ephemeral

4. The new evidence will _____ our argument in the upcoming trial.
   a) obfuscate  b) bolster  c) disparage  d) eschew

5. The _____ of car horns and construction noise made concentration impossible.
   a) cacophony  b) candor  c) conundrum  d) acumen

6. She tried to _____ her reluctant brother into joining the family vacation.
   a) cajole  b) disparage  c) admonish  d) abrogate

7. His refreshing _____ during the interview impressed the hiring committee.
   a) cacophony  b) candor  c) conundrum  d) acumen

8. After weeks of resistance, the city was forced to _____ to the enemy's demands.
   a) abrogate  b) admonish  c) capitulate  d) cajole

9. The _____ weather made it impossible to plan outdoor activities with confidence.
   a) capricious  b) abstruse  c) intractable  d) circumspect

10. Given the sensitive nature of the negotiations, the diplomat remained _____ in his statements.
    a) deleterious  b) capricious  c) circumspect  d) intractable

11. After the heated argument, he adopted a _____ tone to restore peace.
    a) conciliatory  b) deleterious  c) perfunctory  d) hackneyed

12. The detective faced a perplexing _____ with no obvious solution in sight.
    a) quandary  b) acumen  c) conundrum  d) candor

13. His _____ examination of the document missed several critical errors.
    a) cursory  b) thorough  c) meticulous  d) comprehensive

14. The _____ beauty of cherry blossoms makes their brief blooming season even more precious.
    a) ephemeral  b) copious  c) vociferous  d) cursory

15. The movie's _____ plot failed to engage audiences looking for original storytelling.
    a) hackneyed  b) deleterious  c) conciliatory  d) intractable

16. The politician tried to _____ the facts to avoid taking responsibility for the scandal.
    a) capitulate  b) obfuscate  c) disparage  d) abrogate

17. Her _____ apology lacked sincerity and failed to address the real issues.
    a) precocious  b) capricious  c) perfunctory  d) circumspect

18. The ethical _____ left the committee struggling to reach a decision.
    a) quandary  b) acumen  c) conundrum  d) candor

19. The _____ protests outside the courthouse could be heard from several blocks away.
    a) vociferous  b) ephemeral  c) copious  d) cursory

20. The new government decided to _____ the controversial treaty signed by its predecessor.
    a) abrogate  b) disparage  c) eschew  d) bolster
