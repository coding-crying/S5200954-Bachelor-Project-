PART A: FILL IN THE BLANK
Instructions: Fill in the blank with the correct vocabulary word from your learning session.

WORD BANK: abrogate | candor | austere | abstruse | deleterious | capitulate | disparage | vociferous | admonish | copious | conciliatory | hackneyed | garrulous | obfuscate | capricious | eschew | ephemeral | intractable | quandary | circumspect

1. The new government decided to _____ the controversial treaty signed by its predecessor.
   Answer: _______ (correct: abrogate)

2. His refreshing _____ during the interview impressed the hiring committee.
   Answer: _______ (correct: candor)

3. The monastery's _____ living conditions reflected the monks' commitment to simplicity.
   Answer: _______ (correct: austere)

4. The professor's _____ explanation of quantum mechanics left most students confused.
   Answer: _______ (correct: abstruse)

5. The _____ effects of pollution on marine life became increasingly evident.
   Answer: _______ (correct: deleterious)

6. After weeks of resistance, the city was forced to _____ to the enemy's demands.
   Answer: _______ (correct: capitulate)

7. Rather than offer constructive criticism, he chose to _____ his opponent's achievements.
   Answer: _______ (correct: disparage)

8. The _____ protests outside the courthouse could be heard from several blocks away.
   Answer: _______ (correct: vociferous)

9. The teacher had to _____ the students for their disruptive behavior during the assembly.
   Answer: _______ (correct: admonish)

10. The researcher took _____ notes during the lengthy interview session.
   Answer: _______ (correct: copious)

11. After the heated argument, he adopted a _____ tone to restore peace.
   Answer: _______ (correct: conciliatory)

12. The movie's _____ plot failed to engage audiences looking for original storytelling.
   Answer: _______ (correct: hackneyed)

13. The _____ passenger talked non-stop throughout the entire flight.
   Answer: _______ (correct: garrulous)

14. The politician tried to _____ the facts to avoid taking responsibility for the scandal.
   Answer: _______ (correct: obfuscate)

15. The _____ weather made it impossible to plan outdoor activities with confidence.
   Answer: _______ (correct: capricious)

16. Health-conscious consumers increasingly _____ processed foods in favor of natural alternatives.
   Answer: _______ (correct: eschew)

17. The _____ beauty of cherry blossoms makes their brief blooming season even more precious.
   Answer: _______ (correct: ephemeral)

18. The _____ dispute had resisted all attempts at resolution for over a decade.
   Answer: _______ (correct: intractable)

19. Faced with two equally unappealing options, she found herself in a difficult _____.
   Answer: _______ (correct: quandary)

20. Given the sensitive nature of the negotiations, the diplomat remained _____ in his statements.
   Answer: _______ (correct: circumspect)
