PART A: FILL IN THE BLANK
Instructions: Fill in the blank with the correct vocabulary word from your learning session.

WORD BANK: austere | bolster | hackneyed | ephemeral | deleterious | candor | cacophony | abstruse | conundrum | capricious | garrulous | cursory | capitulate | conciliatory | admonish | circumspect | vociferous | cajole | quandary | perfunctory

1. The monastery's _____ living conditions reflected the monks' commitment to simplicity.
   Answer: _______ (correct: austere)

2. The positive reviews helped _____ confidence in the company's new product line.
   Answer: _______ (correct: bolster)

3. The movie's _____ plot failed to engage audiences looking for original storytelling.
   Answer: _______ (correct: hackneyed)

4. The _____ beauty of cherry blossoms makes their brief blooming season even more precious.
   Answer: _______ (correct: ephemeral)

5. The _____ effects of pollution on marine life became increasingly evident.
   Answer: _______ (correct: deleterious)

6. His refreshing _____ during the interview impressed the hiring committee.
   Answer: _______ (correct: candor)

7. The _____ of car horns and construction noise made conversation impossible.
   Answer: _______ (correct: cacophony)

8. The professor's _____ explanation of quantum mechanics left most students confused.
   Answer: _______ (correct: abstruse)

9. The detective faced a perplexing _____ with no obvious solution in sight.
   Answer: _______ (correct: conundrum)

10. The _____ weather made it impossible to plan outdoor activities with confidence.
   Answer: _______ (correct: capricious)

11. The _____ passenger talked non-stop throughout the entire flight.
   Answer: _______ (correct: garrulous)

12. A _____ glance at the report revealed several obvious errors.
   Answer: _______ (correct: cursory)

13. After weeks of resistance, the city was forced to _____ to the enemy's demands.
   Answer: _______ (correct: capitulate)

14. After the heated argument, he adopted a _____ tone to restore peace.
   Answer: _______ (correct: conciliatory)

15. The teacher had to _____ the students for their disruptive behavior during the assembly.
   Answer: _______ (correct: admonish)

16. Given the sensitive nature of the negotiations, the diplomat remained _____ in his statements.
   Answer: _______ (correct: circumspect)

17. The _____ protests outside the courthouse could be heard from several blocks away.
   Answer: _______ (correct: vociferous)

18. She tried to _____ her reluctant brother into joining the family vacation.
   Answer: _______ (correct: cajole)

19. Faced with two equally unappealing options, she found herself in a difficult _____.
   Answer: _______ (correct: quandary)

20. Her _____ apology lacked sincerity and failed to address the real issues.
   Answer: _______ (correct: perfunctory)
