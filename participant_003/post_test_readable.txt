   Answer: _______ (correct: austere)

   Answer: _______ (correct: obfuscate)

   D) deleterious
   Answer: _______ (correct: conciliatory)

   A) abstruse
   B) deleterious
   C) ephemeral
   D) perfunctory
   Answer: _______ (correct: abstruse)

10. The teacher had to _____ the students for their disruptive behavior during the assembly.
   A) cajole
   B) admonish
   C) capitulate
   D) bolster
   Answer: _______ (correct: admonish)

11. The _____ of car horns and construction noise made conversation impossible.
   A) cacophony
   B) candor
   C) quandary
   D) acumen
   Answer: _______ (correct: cacophony)

   A) quandary
   B) conundrum
   C) cacophony
   D) acumen
   Answer: _______ (correct: quandary)

13. The _____ effects of pollution on marine life became increasingly evident.
   A) conciliatory
   B) abstruse
   C) hackneyed
   D) deleterious
   Answer: _______ (correct: deleterious)

14. The _____ passenger talked non-stop throughout the entire flight.
   A) copious
   B) austere

   A) eschew

   A) conciliatory
   B) intractable
   C) deleterious

   A) austere
   B) garrulous

   D) vociferous

   A) intractable
